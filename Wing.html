Wing Project

This was the final project for my engineering design class which culminated of material research and comparison, stress analysis, CAD, and milling (both manual and CNC). The goals of the project were to construct the lightest wing possible, with minimum span of 23 inches, while also minimizing delfection under a 90 pound load.

    Wing Final Desgin Review

My Role

My primary role consisted of generated the GCode code needed to run the CNC mill, which was completed in Creo Parametric 10, set up the mill by setting the coordinate axes, zeroing, and inputing tools, and then manualy milling anything in which the program could not get to. My secondary role was to assit in the simulation and analysis of the wing, also done in Creo Parametric 10. This consisted of running programs of different wing shapes and internal structures and comparing results to figure out which is the best for our given requirements.
